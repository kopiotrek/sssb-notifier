Available apartments

					
Do you meet our living conditions? If the answer is yes, you are welcome to apply for an accommodation.

Read more about our different types of accommodations and housing areas.


You can book a maximum of three accommodations during each publication period.



  If you have an admission letter for the autumn semester 2025, you are warmly welcome to apply for housing with a start date of August 1 or later. To apply for housing starting before August, we require a certificate of registration and active student union membership for the spring semester 2025.
